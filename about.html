About Janvier Jewelers

Brothers Ed and Lee Janviér founded Janviér Jewelers in 1975 on the principle of providing superior customer service. An independent, family-owned business, Janviér Jewelers' success is a direct result of over 37 years of building and maintaining customer loyalty. We are proud to say that many of the relationships we have with our customers span several generations. We have the distinct pleasure of continuing to provide service to our loyal patrons because of the importance we place on treating our customers like family.

Providing customers with the absolute best jewelry buying experience means that the products we offer also need to be the absolute best. Unlike most jewelry stores, we hand select our diamonds direct from cutters from around the world in order to ensure that our showroom is complete with the highest quality selection available.

Our dedication to outstanding customer service shows in the experience of our staff. At Janviér Jewelers, our associates have an average of 25 years of extensive fine jewelry expertise, including gemological education. With certifications from the Gemological Institute of America in Diamond Grading, Advanced Fine Jewelry Sales, Fine Jewelry Consultants, Detecting Synthetic Diamonds, Identifying Challenging Synthetics, Gem Identification, and Color Stone Grading, our team is committed to upholding high standards of excellence.

At Janviér Jewelers, we aren't just looking to make another sale; we're looking to make another lifetime customer. Visit our Christiana Mall location today to see for yourself why so many rely on our quality, service and trust for the ultimate jewelry buying experience.
